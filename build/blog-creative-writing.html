Dante's Trip

Saturday, June 29th, 2019

I wrote a story called Dante's Trip while taking creative writing courses at Stanford. It was a story about psychedelics, hedonism, and morality. It drew a large inspiration from my all time favorite book, Dante Alighieri's classic allegory: The Inferno. I intended to finish the story, but for various reasons - the biggest being that I've lost the source material - it has gone unfinished. I still intend to finish this story, and when I do, I will post it here. Probably.
